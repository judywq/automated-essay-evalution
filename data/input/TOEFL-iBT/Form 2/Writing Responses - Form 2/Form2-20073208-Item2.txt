As the world develps, the societies become more crowded with people, and there is more interaction and communication between them.  Because of this, the ability to cooperate well with others is more important than it was in the past. There are several reasons.

The first reason can be shown by an interesting statistics. According to a survey done by a company in the United States, people nowadays are attracted more to "fun" people than they do to others with different personal traits. Being fun means that the person can entertain and cooperate well with the others; therefore, by having that ability, one can be more succussful at his/her profession than others, since the clients, bosses, or parters will prefer to have him/her.

The second reason is the fact that most of universities or cooperations in the world are now looking for people with great leaderships; in other words, they look for those who can work with and direct people well. This personal trait is very important, because now, unlike the past, many companies have clients for many different regions. The companies must have more workers as a result, therefore being able to control all these workers has become crucial for someone in the higher position in the company.

The last reason is the that the aspect of work has changed from the past. In the past, most of the companies were usually local-based; in other words, they only dealt with customers in the restricted region.  As the world grow, however, companies now has to deal with more clients, who can be in various of regions, or even in different countries. In order to do business with these clients, one must be able to satisfy and cooperate well with those customers.

These three reasons show that the ability to cooperate well with others is far more important than it was in the past. One who has this ability will be successful at anything he/she intends to do.
