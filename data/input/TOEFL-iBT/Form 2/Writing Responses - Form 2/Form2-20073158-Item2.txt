There are many ways that the topic of the ability to cooperate with others can be approached. Some people think that  the ability to cooperate is very important. Others disagree. I have my own point of view as well. I prefer that the ability to cooperate  with others is important for two reasons.
First of all, almost every job is one samll piece of a big project in our society. We almost can not complet a thing independently. Take me, for example. I am a physical therapist. Before I treat a patient with knee probms, I need a x-ray examination to sure the diagnosis of the patient. Then, I can set an appropriate treatment program. However, I am not good at radiology. I can't take a x-ray by my own. Hence, I have to cooperate with other specialists well that I can treat this patient properly. This is a good example that the ability to cooperate well with others is far more importan than it in the past.
Secondly, today's society  is different from the past. People can't not live alone nowadays. Take transportation, for example. In the past, people could transport by waling, but the distance was short. But now, there are a lot alterantives for transportation. People can take bus or metro to a far place. The most important things that you have to cooperate with others, such as drivers, that you can use these tools. Such example shows that the cooperation with others is more important than past.  
Of course, there is an argument to be made for other opinions. However, for the reasons that I have presented above, I feel that my opinion is the most valid.
