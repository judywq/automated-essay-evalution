	In today's world, the ability to cooperate well with others is far more important than it was in the past.  Unlike in the past, when the strong party always won, in today's world, everybody has to win. A simple trading example, years ago sellers had power over buyers. Sellers determined prices, services, times of delivery while buyers did not have the power but to buy from the same sellers everytime. However, in today's world where technology speeds up information, buyers can buy products from different sellers. If sellers do not treat buyers right, buyers can always find other sellers to buy from. 
Not to mention that we now have BBB where buyers can complain about sellers if they are not being treated right. 
	Another reason that people have to cooperate with each other is that in today's world, works are related to other department. Cooperating well with all deparments will help company to run and jobs to get done. Just imagine about a hardware company whose engineering department does not want to cooperate with marketing department. Engineer department will be able to produce new products but there will be no income for the company because the marketing department do not want to sell the products. As a result, company will not survive in the long run. Both engineer and marketing departments will loose their jobs. In the past, this was not a matter because there were not as many companies or industries as they are now.  People used to make things by hand and sold their products in a small nearby market. They did not depend to each other as much as they do now.   
	In short, people have to be able to cooperate well with others because they need each other. Without cooperation, business will not run.
