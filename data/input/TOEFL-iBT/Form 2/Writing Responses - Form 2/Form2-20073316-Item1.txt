The lecture means that fish farming is actually good for local wild fish. He means that often the local wild fish were endangered by over fishing and that an end to fishing these will help get the population back to earlier levels. He means that this benefit is greater than the risk of spreading diseases. 
	The second point he discusses is that you should not compare farmed fish with wild fish but with other farmed meat. He means that other kinds of meat like chicken or pork also get fed with chemicals and that in general fish is better than pork or chicken in terms of fat and other aspects.
	Finaly he says that feeding fish with fish food is a good thing. This is because usually the fish used to feed other fish is fish not edible by humans. He means that by feeding some fish with non edible fish we create more edible fish, which he thinks is the most inmportant thing.
