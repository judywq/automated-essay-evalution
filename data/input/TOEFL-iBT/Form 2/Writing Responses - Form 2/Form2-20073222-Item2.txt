	Thanks to the technology progresses which have been invented for some decades, life became much convenient for people to live independently, and the importance of the ability to cooperate well with others decreased.
	As the first reason, it can be considered that it became easier for people to do something has to be done by themselves  For example, at the manufacturing line in a factry, people can complete their task by themselves by using electric devices such as automated manufacturing system. In contrast, people had to cooperate with each other to complete their task in the dress-wear factory twenty years ago. Technological progress not only increased the effectiveness of the manufacturing but also decreased the dependancies of each employees, and also reduced the importance the ability to cooperate with each other.
	The other reason is that computer related technologies, especially the progress of the internet dramatically reduced the importance of the ability to cooperate well with each other. In the past, for instance; when people wanted to go shopping, and if he or she did not want to go alone, he or she had to find someone who could be with him/her, or he/she had to give up if he/she could not find anyone with him/her. However, now people can buy anything through the internet world by themselves without any partners. This technological progress make it possible for people to do everthing wants 
	In these regards, although the relationship between others sttll might be important in terms of communications, the ability to cooperate with others can be said less important than it was in the past.
