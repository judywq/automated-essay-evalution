In today's world, the ability to cooperate well with others is far important than it was in the past. Cooperation will make strong influences on your success. Everyone, every company, every business have their own advantages and disadvantages. If you know how to cooperate well, your business, your job, and even your life will get much better. Using your strengh to help other people and asking other people to use their strengh to help you, you can get many benefits from that mutual cooperation. 
First example is business cooperation. If you can product a large amount of chickens but you don't have branch name in marketing, you can not sell your products with a large amount of consumption. Another company have been very famous in selling chicken products but they don't have enough products to sell to custumers. Then you can sell your products to them. By that way, you can improve your consumption and another company have products to sell their customer. 
The second example is that Federal Express and USPS cooperate with each other to delivery  mails or gifts to their customers very quickly. Federal Express give USPS some plots on their planes, so USPS can bring priority mails, first class customers, important products to customer very quickly without being charged by Federal Express. In exchange, USPS lets FED_EX leave their boxs at 1000 post offices. USPS is the neighbor to every states in USA, so all FED-EX's boxes will be deliveried to their customer very fast and easy.

Therefore, cooperation is very impotant. The developments of societies, business, technology, and science improve very rapidly because human being know how to make the cooperation more effective and flexible in any situation.
