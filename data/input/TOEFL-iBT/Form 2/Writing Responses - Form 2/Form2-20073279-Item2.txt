Cooperation have always being a key factor in a lot of situations. 
Every work, project is not likely to have good results without  a cooperation from all the members of a group.

Prehistorical men had to cooperate between each others. In order to eat an apropriate and varied menu, some of them were charged to hunt and some others had to recolect food from plants. Nowadays, we have now the same problems, that is why not every people has the same job. By doing that we can help others providing them with what they need and we can offer, and having a reciprocal treatment.

In addition, we are in the word of globalisation. We can go to France and eat in an american restaurant, or be in United States and go to an italian one. We can take a plane, and be in the oposite side of the world in less than 24 hours. Is here cooperation important?

From my point of view, since now we have to work with people from all over the world, one of the most important qualifications a man or woman should have is to be cooperative. It is more and more common a group formed with different persons who came from different countries and who have different backgrounds and cultures.
