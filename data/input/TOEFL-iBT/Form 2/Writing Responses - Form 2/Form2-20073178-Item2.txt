A question which often appears in today's live where everyone is only focused on his personal success, is,  if the ability to cooperate well with others is nowadays more important than it was in the past.
I think a good cooperation is always very important; in business life as well as in a friendship or a marriage.
But as far I can see, in a friendship or a marriage, cooperation was in the past just as important as it is today. I want to concentrate myself on the cooperation in business life. I think a lot of things have changed here. It used to be that when you bought or sold goods, you only had a few people who sold and a few custumers who bought goods. Mostly habitants of your village or a neighbour village. You had to cooperate with them, because if they didn't agree with you, or you didn't agree with them, you weren't able to make a deal and could not afford yourdelf a living. 
But nowadays, where you can contact every part of the world and are able to deliver goods to any place on earth, cooperation is not so important as it used to be. If the local custumer won't exept your offer, you might find someone on the other side of the world who is glad about your offer. So due to the fact that the earth is getting smaller and smaller, cooperation in business is not so important anymore. 
But you have to find partner which will deliver your goods to their destination, and with them, cooperation is important again.
So in the conclusion, i think cooperation always is very important and should never be forgotten. Especially between friends and long business contacts. Cooperation is one of the most important fact of success.
