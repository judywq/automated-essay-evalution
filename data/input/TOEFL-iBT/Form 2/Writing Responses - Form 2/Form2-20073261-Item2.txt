We need Cooperations

If we are talking about cooperation, I would like give you one example first. I used to work in the clothing industry. I know how people did in the past. In the begining when people knew how to make a dress, say, thousands of years ago, they used to make it by themselves. Later on, craftsman appeared. The skilled dressmaker would make the whole dress by him or herself, from designing, cutting to assembling. What happens now? Hundreds of people will be involved in this dress making. From the colorists, designers, manufactuering workers, distributers and so on. Even in the manufactuer line, there are dozens of people working in different parts of the dress.

Here is the point I am going to make,  cooperation is much more important than it was in the past. In the past, there are some masters who knew every aspects of a thing, such as buildig a house, But now, it would be difficult for a man to know, not mention to do, the whole building things alone. Maybe it is because the task was relatively simple, and the requirement of the task was not so high. But as you may see now, as the development of society, people are more concerned  with the detail. Detail shows difference. Detail shows quality. Thus the distinction of the job assignment is so great that no one can possibly know every detail of this job. Cooperation is needed! I do one part of the job, and you do the other part, in this way the work is done, as stated in an old saying popular in my culture: three ordinary people are wiser than a master.
  
What if you do not have the ability to cooperate? Which means, you only focus on your job, you never lend your hand to others, and others won't help you either, you do your work alone.
