The modern world is very complex. Necessary abilities to succeed in today's world have changed.
In my oppinion, the ability to cooperate well with others has become very important. A lot of companies have changed their vision of the perfect work environment. Nowdays every company looks at a person's ability to be a good team player before cosidering one for a job opening. As we know, there are different strategies for decision making in the modern business world. A lot companies find group decision making more effective than individual decision making. The idea is that  people form diferent departments of the company have diferent knowledges that can be useful in decision making. Every individual should be able to express one's idea on a specific topic and,  more importantly, to come to an agreement on a decision with his or hers co-workers. So that being said, nowdays an individual has to have a good ability to cooprate with the others. 
I would like to give an example from my personal experience as well to demonstrate the importance of the ability to cooperate with the others. My work team consists of a  few people. We all wok well together and help each other as needed. I work in high spead environment that requires the ability to handle a few tasks at the same time. There are some days that somebody's help is needed tremendously, then I have my co-worker steping up to the plate. All togethere we can accomplish any task, and satisfy our customers. It is extremely important for people to be able to work well together, in another words, cooperate with others. 
To summarize the above, it is hard to state that in today's world the ability to cooperate well with others is far more important than it was in the past, but it is certainly plays very important part in modern world.
