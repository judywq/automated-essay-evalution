
		The Importance of the Ability to Cooperate


	Now days, an important ability people needs to develop is the ability to cooperate. It is a very useful ability because we are living in a world were we depend on each other a lot. There are very few people that can actually provide everything they need for themselves. In the world today, every one has a special job to do and that makes tasks very narrow. In the development stage in wich we are, there is what we call job distribution;  it means there are thousands different jobs and people everyday concentrate more in  specific topics. 
 	In that order of ideas, our everyday life makes us depend on each other and in the same way cooperate with each other. If the person who is in charge of milking the cow, does not do it, the one who has to sell it, won't have anything to sell; and the one that consumes it, won't have anything to drink. This example may sound a little extreme, but in large scale that is how it really works. 
	Everyone contributes by the compliance of their own job, wich would make posible the other's job. That is a very important way to cooperate with others, and the interdependence that has grown through time is what makes it so important. 
	It's true that for all societies cooperation has been very important in many different levels. But not as important as it is now. In early days, for people to survive, there were not as many specific tasks as they are now. The lifestyle we sustain makes coopertion with others a priority. When people didn't need all the goods we need these days to carry out a certain lifestyle, they could provide them for themselves. It meant they didn't need much from others and it wasn't very importatn to cooperate with each other.   
	To conclude, we could't live the life we do if we would't have the ability to cooperate with each other.
