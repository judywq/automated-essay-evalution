I think that in today's world the ability to cooperate well with others is more important than it was in the past.
To support this statement I can give some reasons.
Firstly you can see the world today. There is many competitions between people and between companies, but to have success you can't work alone!
I want to explain the idea with some examples. 
If I think about great firms, I see that are very different from old firms.
Now people work in teams. Some firms create inside task-force. Sharing different skills and experiences between workers is essential  to make some improvement.
The objective of a firm is not only a pesonal objective of the owner but should become a objective for all the workers.
Another example it's the relationship with suppliers and customers. 
Today if you want to achieve great results, you should cooperate with these figures.
In the past there was no trust. The customer and the supplier was totally external to the production. Now, customers needs, are very important, and they are often the start points for choose a new product.
Also in a family  cooperation is foundamental.
Now it's likely that both members of the family have a job.
So it's very important to share the activities inside a family: such as houseworks, looking after children, and every other dutie.
In conclusion I think that today, the world seems to be populate by selfish people, but to reach the objectives and to have a good life it's necessary to cooperate.
