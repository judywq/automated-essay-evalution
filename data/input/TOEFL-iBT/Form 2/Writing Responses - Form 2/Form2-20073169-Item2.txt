    The ability to cooperate is extremely crucial for today's world.  Projects and works of nowaday society is often larger in scale and higher in complexity than ever.  Additionally, the time given to accomplish an assignment is also shortened.

Take cell phone industry as an example.  Nowadays, the life span of a cell phone on the market is way shorter than it was a few years ago. To both make enough profit and produce high quality that meets the need of consumers, good ability to cooperate is without questioning the most important event.
