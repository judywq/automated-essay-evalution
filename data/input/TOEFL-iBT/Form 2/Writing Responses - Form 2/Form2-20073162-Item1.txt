     The topic is mainly about the negative and positive issues of fish farming.Fish farming is a way of commercial fish production.
     One  negative issue of fish farming is that it pose some health risks to commercially grown fish.Since they are crowded in a small area, risks of developing infections and diseases are possible to spread to the human population. Another problem with fish farming is that it tends to create health issues to the human population with the use of chemicals to promote growth of the fish population. Third negative issue is the use of wild fish to fed as a fish meal on all farmed fish.
     On the positive side, traditional fishing is the main cause of decrease fish population and not farmed fishing. Instead it helps to improve the growth of the fish polulation. On the issue of chemicals, there are other producers aside from fish farming that use a lot of chemicals to feed like the poultry, beef ,and pork. Farmed fishing have a little consumption of chemicals as compared to the others mentioned. Commercially grown fish do not eat wild fish that are normally eaten by humans.What is important is that the main goal of farmed fishing is to increase the number of edible fish rather than to decrease it.
