The lectures rebuts the three points against fish-farming made in the passage:
1. Fish farming was a natural consequence of overfishing and depletion of the wild fish stock. So it is for replenishing not depleting the wild fish stock. The fish farms were created to help increase stocks of wild fish not deplete them by introduction of diseases and parasites. The latter does happen, albeit,  the net is positive in terms of greater number of fish stocks being produced and restored. 
2. The passage erroneously compares farm fish to wild fish. The same chemicals used for growth -inducing in fish are also used in poultry, beef etc. The important difference is that they are used in smaller amounts compared to those in these other food types. 
3. Using Fish meal does not lead to a net negative protein balance because fish meal is made using inedible fish and feeding fish meal on fish farms leads to creating more edible fish that can be consumed.
