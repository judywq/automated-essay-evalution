No matter in nowadays  or in the past, people need  lots of ablitities to attain  success, to solve problem and  etc. which include cooperation, competition,leading power and so on.
However, In today's world the ablity to cooperate well with others is truly more important that it was in the past. 
Firstly, only  one can cooperate, he can get his personal success. Today's world is different with the one in the past. In golden old days, a man can enjoy the triumpy fruit  even without others' help.For example , a writer could did a huge-amount survey and wrote his masterpiece alone.But in nowadays ,people are surrounded by floods of information and materials .No one can imagine a big survey such as Gallop  census were conducted by only one person which may cost that poor man a life time to finish it.So in such a fast-developing world , people can only get the acme of his life through others'help.
Secondly, only a nation or a world can cooperate , they can solve the problem they face.Nowadays , human beings suffer from the dangers of air pollution,climate crisis, energy crisis ,conflict and wars between nations.No governments ,organizations or individuals can claim that they can find the key to those problems.Everybody know clearly only through the cooperation , people can handle this position.But in the past ,humans did not have the knowledge of the global dilemma we face today and even if they had , they  could solve it only by cooperation.  
So in my opinion, to cooperate well with others is more important for human being,not for himself but also for the whole world.To cooperate well means one have more chance to win, more friends he can depend on ,more dynamic power with which he can fight for his goal.
