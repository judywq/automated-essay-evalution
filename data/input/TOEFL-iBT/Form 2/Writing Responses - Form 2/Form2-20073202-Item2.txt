	With technology, the ability of instant communcation has been brought to the normal citizen as much as the potencial to collaborate more with others. This brings the fact that we are electronically in touch with much more people than we would be able to in the past. But technology itself does not bring cooperation, therefore one must develop the ability to cooperate well with others if this person wants to be successful in professional life. There are even studies that show that people with better cooperation abilities have more chances of having promotions and better sallaries. So this should not be neglected by anyone, specially when they have all the modern tools to apply their cooperation skills better than in the past.
	But cooperation does not limit itself to professional life. We are seeing many countries having serious social and economic problems because they refuse to cooperate with their neighbours, in many cases historical rivals. This only create prejudice and hate, things that does not benefit anyone. We as a global society have to ban this kinds of bad behaviours, and one form of doing so is by cooperating with others.
	Cooperation is also important in your community. If more people actively participate in their own comunity, life quality would be higher for more people. It's a fact that cooperation in community was more common in the past, today people seem to be living in their own cocoon and sometimes forget that they are living surrounded by others. 
	Cooperation should be taught to kids, it's certainly necessary in social life and also teaches to avoid bad attitudes. A world with more cooperation is a happier world to everyone, and if we are building a better world to our kids, we shall not forget to cooperate between us and teaching the younger ones to do so.
