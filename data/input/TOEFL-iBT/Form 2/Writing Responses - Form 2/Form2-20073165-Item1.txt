According to the lecture, there are three points why fish farming is not harmful as the writer of the passage believed.
	First, fish farming helps local species to rebound. People who usually go to the coastals to fish, can now go fishing in the fish farming. Thus, coastals will not be destroyed and local species will rebound. 
	Second, all of food products people consume contain growth-inducing chemicals. This will include poultry, beef and fish. Compared to beef and poultry, fish meat is much more beneficial for human body because fish has less fat. Fish favor people health.
	Third, fish farming does not use common wild fish for fish food. The fish used to feed the fish in fish farming come from fish that are not normally eaten by people. With that said, fish food for fish farming does not reduce the amount of protein available from the sea.
