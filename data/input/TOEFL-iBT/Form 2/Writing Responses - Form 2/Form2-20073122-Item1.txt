   Fish farming, which started, in 1960s, is becoming the main source of fish that we eat today. It is said that one third of the fish we eat today are grown from the farm. The reading passage, however, claims that it is not a good idea to continue fish farming, since it brings up many problems, while lecturer says there is no harm at all.

 First idea that makes problem, according to the reading, is that fishfarming threatens the marine life that live close to the farm. Since there are limited spaces for the fishes to grow, there are many diseases created. Lecturer, on the other hand, says that it is true that it may influence other living orgamisms, it is more crucial to save the live of the fishes, since they are in danger of extinction.

 Another is that farmers often make fish eat growth inducing chemicals in order to make them grow quicker, and fatter. It may affect directly to human health. Professor rebuts that not only fish farmers use them, but other farm raised products are using them too, It is not only fish that contains the substances.

 Last opinion of the reading is that it is associated with the long term wasting process, because farm fish are fed with fish meal, food made by using wild fish. However, farmers never use the edible fishes to make fish meals, but they use fishes that humans cannot eat, which is not related to wasting.
