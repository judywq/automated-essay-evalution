In this set of materials, the reading passage discribes the problems of fish farming and gives three advantages. However in the listening passage the professor opposites these three points.

In order to be against the problem that fish farming is dangerous to the health of wild fish, the professor tells the students that the most dangerous activity to wild fish is commercial fishing. And the fish farming is a useful way to protect wild fish. By fish farming, people can eat the fish which is raised by famers and reduce the consumption of wild fish.

The professor also provides his oppinion against the point that farm-raised fish exposes human consumers to a health risk. Taking into account the consumption of other meat such as beef, people eat less fish. All animals raised by man are often fed by growth-inducing chemicals. It is unreasonable to blame fish farming. On the other hand fish has more nutrition and protein and less fat than other meat. It is healthy to human being.

At last the professor points that now fish famers have changed the fish meal. People should not be worry about the waste of fish food.
