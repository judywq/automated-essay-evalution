	Since the 1960s, fish farming has become one of the commercial fish production. Now it is found that fish farming brings few of negative consequences and should be discontinued.
	At first, fish farming causses danger to wild fish. The most important danger is the competion of food. Farm-raised fish with a huge numbers reduce a lot of protein available from the sea. Fish farming are also raised  in a certain small area. It is a good condition to develop disease and parastic infection. Those disease spread through water will affect wild fish which was supposed to harm. Traditional company who often produce wild fish product will be affected.
	Second, farm-raised fish is often raised with a lot of chemicals as growth-inducing chemicals or chemicals to treat with disease. Besides, Farm-raised fish is one of resources of nuitrience for other farm-raised animals. Its disease and chemicals have more opportunity to spread out. As the result, the last consumers of all those farm-raised product are human. It is possible to say that fish farming increase the risk of human health.
