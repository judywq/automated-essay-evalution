	I'm sure that nowadays, the fact of cooperating with other people is much more important than it was a couple of centuries ago. The reason is that we are now in the comunication era, and the whole world is linked. For this reason there's no point in triying to work on a project or produce something by your own because if you're producing, it's not for yourself but for someone else. That's why it's extremly important to learn to comunicate and learn to cooperate with others in order to have some kind of benefit. This statement is becoming more and more like a law, a law that is necessary to survive in a world moved by the industry. 
	If we move to the past we'll observe that this need has appeared recently and that it way unexistant before. A couple of centuries ago, the mentality of the people was very different from now. There was no need to cooperate with someone else because the political system of the age was not as open as now. Things started to change at the end of the middle age, after Cristofer Columbus discovered the american continent, at that moment, people discovered that there was something beyond the individual country or continent, but this mentality took a long time to develop itself. The next stage came with the end of the french revolution, when nacionalists started to vinculate to other countries to form agreements. Finally the XIXth century brought with itself the fist industrial revolution which ended developing this mentality. From that moment people developed an unexplicable need to cooperate an comunicate with the others. This natural instinct which appeared as a primitive impulse that has been sharpened by the time and transformed in an universal need which nowadays we call cooperation.
