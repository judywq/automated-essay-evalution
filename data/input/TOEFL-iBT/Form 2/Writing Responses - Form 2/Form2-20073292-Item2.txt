Cooperation is what my parents or teacher or even some old sayings always told me to keep in mind. Such like that one man is never do bettter than two brains. However, I sure see some very successful  person that doesn't take cooperation so much importance, or to say that things work well and even better with their own self. So I really think that time is moving and changing, and that the great importance of cooperation has been gone so far. This essay discusses more reasons in the following.

First, I think that it is the way people live has changed that change how we weigh the value of cooperation. Back in history, humans lived in a world that count on agriculture or hunting. So people must be unified to do the work together. For instance, when in my grandmother's time, whole family live closely togerther for the reason that the workload in farming is really heavy, not one can independently finish those work. Work needs everyone in the family to help done. And  it is the same for the whole village or tribe because water resources and soil those core ingredients to agriculture or hunting and they can't divide apart form family to family. However, look at our everyday work. My father and mother go to work separately in two different company, and my sister and I go to school differently. We don't need to cooperate to make our living any more.

Second, we have the greatest advanced technology nowadays. Computers and Internet can help us do a lot of things. We can just stay at home and make all the stuff done, not even going out. We can surf the Internet for lots of information, and we longer need other's help which ask for help face to face or by the phone. We go for the net, and find the answer all by our own. We can independently make our work done.   

That is my opinion why cooperation is not so important after all.
