Much work, such as doing business or building, need cooperation. Nowadays, the world is developing so fast that the work sometimes is more difficult than the one in the past. So I think it's more crucial for us to be able to cooperate well with other people now.
First, some work must be done by many people. Take the Olmpic Games for example. Without good cooperation, to hold such a huge event which involves hundreds of countries and regions, and millions of people allover the world is almost impossible. There was the Olympic Games too in ancient Greece.But it is so small compared to the one today. And to be taken place successfully, the one in the world today is more difficult and needs more cooperation.  
In addition,cooperation is more likely to happen nowadays.Since in today's world , the tools to communicate and the vehicles to travel have developed so well that people are much less separated, the possibility for us to work with other people is far bigger than that in the past.  If you are working in a foreign company and are going to do a project with your colleagues, you must have a good ability to  work together with others.
In a word, the ability to work together well with others is much more critical than it was in the old days. Therefore, people must improve this ability of their own if they determine to work better.
