The speaker does not believe that fish farming brings a number of harmful consequences and should be discontinued as the reading says. He supports his opinion by listing three points. First, he says that fish farming is not the major reason why the wild fish is in danger but the commercial fishing is. And it would not make a big difference whether it exists or not. On the other hand, it actually protects the wild fish since it can feed the need of the market. If people stop fish farming, the population of the wild fish will only decrease. Second, he discusses the issue about the chemicals used in fish farming. He points out the fact that pork, beef and many other kinds of meat also contain different kinds of chemicals. And more important is, compared to pork and beef, fish meat has less fat and thus it is more healthier to human body. His third point is that fish farming does not waste the protein from the sea. The fish meal used in fish farming are fish that people do not eat.As a result, it gives us more food available.
