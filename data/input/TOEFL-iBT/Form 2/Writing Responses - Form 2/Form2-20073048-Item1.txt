The problem is that the fish farming brings a lot of consequences that should be stopped. The first problem is that fish farming jeopardizes the health and the enviroment to those wild fished around the farm. Those wild fished could make diseases and parasitic infections happened if this small area relatively enclosed. Even thought that the farmers would try to help the fishes which have been imposed to disease, the water surrounded by farm would still spread out and endanger the species that near the illness farm. Secondly, lots of consumers like fish and accordingly, fish has less fat but lots of nutrition. People would rather buy fish instead of beef or pork. That way those farm-raised fish could impose a serious health risk to people who buy the fish. Also, in order to produce bigger fish faster, farmers often feed their fish with special chemicals.
