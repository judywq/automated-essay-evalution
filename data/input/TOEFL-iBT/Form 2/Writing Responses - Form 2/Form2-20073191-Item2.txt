 "Together, we live. Alone, we die." It is a old saying in my mother country. Most of the old saying still can be applied to nowadays lives, but I disagree with this one. 
 My country has a long history. People had an agriculture-based life for a long time. To sustain good agricultural abundance, the work between the family members were very important.  Therefore, in these times, the number of family members each family possessed was the number one priority. For example, my grandfather has 10 family members. This company like family worked very efficiently. Father, being the master of the family, led the whole entire familiy members. He had control over the family, so the members except the father had to cooperate well in order to effectively accomplish fathers orders. However, family these days are rather very small numbered and live their lives individually unlike the old days. 
  There is also my country's political reason. In the past, my country required people to cooperate and simplify themselves. It was because it was easier for governments to control the people for their needs. In otherwords, people were forced to be cooperate. However, the world has changed, and liberty of individuals are the most important value. Since, people nowdays are open to freedom people has no suppression to cooperate like the past.
  The last reason, educational interest in my country increased like skyscrapers in just several years. Since the national land of my country is very small, people tend to think that only way to success is to be well-educated. However, if any foreigner observes my country these days, only he will see is a everyone competing for their own good rather than cooperating for everyone's good.
  Cooperating ability was the prime value in the past of my country. 
However, technological advace, political growth, and  incresse of educational interest totally changed it. Individuality and liberty if the most important value nowdays. Not cooperation.
