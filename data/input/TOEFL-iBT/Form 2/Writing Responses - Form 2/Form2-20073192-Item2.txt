The cooperation among people has always been very important in the development of a society but in today's world it is far more important than it was in the past.
First of all we can think about farmers in rural enviroments. In the past cooperation was not that important and each farmer produced just what he needed for himself and for his family without giving importance to what other people did. In today's world it's difficult, in my opinion, to think that someone can live just with his own work on the land. People, in this case farmers, have to produce enough products to sell them and gain the money to buy what they need. With no dubt, cooperation among farmers helps them to obtain better price conditions either when they sell their products or buy the things they need for farming.   This example shows how  in today's world the ability to cooperate can help farmers to reach a better and healthier life style.
We can also look at economic and political issues. The cooperation is not only important among people but also among states. Today's world is much more correlated than it was in the past and it is quite normal that problems arise among nations. In my opinion  cooperation instead of isolation or war could better help to overcome these problems.
