  The world we live in has changed dramatically over the past decade. Such changes could be seen clearly in the structure of the society and jobs. Jobs changed to be fit for many people and the society too has also changed to require the hands of many people. Therefor the ability to cooperate well with other became critical than it has been in the past.
  The diviersity of jobs is one of the most significiant reason why we need to cooperate well with other people. Jobs today required many skilled people to do. For example, party designer requires not only him or herself planning, but also helps from other people. They have to brainstorm their ideas and put it together. After that they have to put the items in order, which many people have to pitch in. By having to cooperate with other people, there are more ideas and thoughts, also shortening the time it could be finished.
  Not only is the diversity of jobs significant, but also the structural change in the society. The society has changed to make people work together more frequently. For example, big companies have small groups inside the company itself. That small group is responsible for the work they do and if they fail the work , all the workers in that group will be responsible. Therefore, workers would have to cooperate well with their collegeus to ensure that they will suceed in doing the work. Also, most of the work is made to be fit for many people, creating the ability of cooperation among people.
  The ability to cooperate with others isn't a new aspect in our lifes, but as time has passed, its uses has become critical and pivitol in one's life. The diversity and the structure of the society has changed to have the skills of interacting with others more important than the past.
