I agree with the proposition that the abillity to cooperate well with others is far more important that it was in the past. There are two reasons for me to support it.

One is that no one can master everything by himself or herself in the information age. In the past agriculture age, someone could learn how to sow and leap the crops. As sociery is more complicated, no one can learn everything during his or her lifetime. Everyone has to share his or her knowledge with others to deal with problems. For example, my company has plan to establish new accounting system. The company makes a task-team to do it. One of members is a specialist for software programming. Another member is an accountant. In complicated society, it is impossible for one person to master all things. As the task becomes complicated, the ability to communicat with others is more important than it was int the past.
  
Second is that the ability to cooperate well with others helps him or her to improve his or her knowledge. While communicating with others, he or she can learn what he or she does not think of. In addition, the task in the company requires new creative idea. While communicating well with others, he or she can be inspired. I heard that one who invented MP3 player had the idea through brain storming with others. The brain storming is one of methods to sharing the idea with others. 

In conclusion, the present society became more complicated that in the past. It asks more creative than in the past. As the society has changed, no one can solve the problems by himself or herself. Thus, the ability to cooperate well with others is the primary factor for success.
