Although the passage insists of discontinuing fish farming with three negative points, it is important to see those points from another point of view and to consiering positive effects.  
Firstly, it is not really correct to say that fish farming jeopardizes the wild fish around the farm.  Because it is seen that numbers of wild fish are significantly decreased in costal area by the former common method of traditinal fishing.
Secondly, the passage explain the possibility of a health risk to human consumers due to growth-inducing chemicals included in the feeds.  However, that kind of chemicals are also used to raise beef, pork, and other meats.  When considering of the volume of fish consumption compared to other meats and benefit of the fish like low fat, it is less harmful for consumers.
Thirdly, fish farming does not actually waste the fild fish.  It is true that fish farmers use several pounds of fish meal, but it is a kind of fish which people do not eat.  Thus, farm fishing could save the protein available from the sea.
