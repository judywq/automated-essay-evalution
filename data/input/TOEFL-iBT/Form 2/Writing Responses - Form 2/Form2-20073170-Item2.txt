      Through my daily life experience,  I deeply feel a person should acquire the ability to cooperate well with others and in this increasingly competitive society, this is more important than it was in the past.
     People need to help each other either in living or working. Thus the ability to cooperate with others is vital.  Most important porgress in technology, or the breakthroughs in science, depend on cooperation rather than efforts of some single person.  Building a  well living community, even building  a harmony society, still needs cooperation. Therefore, people should learn to cooperate with others and to cooperate well with others.  
    Comparing with my parents, who have brothers and sisters in their own faminy , I am the only sun. So are all my friends. Many people, including me, lack the time spending with others and this caused the deficiency of ability to cooperate with people. Consequently, people will be isolated from each other. People now make friends by cooperating and depend on each other in their life. So,a persom who need friends should cooperate well wilth others and accordingly, people who have a lot of good friends must be capabl of cooperating well with others.
    I can't not image the situation that I am left alone just because I can not cooperate well with people.
