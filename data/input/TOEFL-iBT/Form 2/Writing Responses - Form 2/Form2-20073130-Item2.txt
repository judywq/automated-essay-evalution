In my opinion it is very important to cooperate with others. It was very important in the past too but I think its more important right now . Here is why I think that way :

First of all, there are a lot of businesses. Most of them have different teams in their companies . Those could be for example construction teams, interior designers, project managers and so on. The point is just, we have to work a lot more in teams then we did before and they have to cooperate and communicate. If they dont , there will be severe problems in the whole company . The people in those teams, have to stay in touch and they have to be all up to date, so they know if there were any changes, new things and so on. And the most important thing, they have to do that all as a team. I think teamwork and cooperation is very important in a business.
Another point is, I think its very important to cooperate with your family as well as in your company. It's kind of like in a business. A family has to be a team or a unit . So here you have cooperate as well. You will have a lot of problems or misunderstanding if you dont communicate with your family. And the thing is you do it every day.
Not just in your family or in a business you cooperate you cooperate with your friends even with strangers as well. If you buy an airplane ticket, for example, you have to cooperate with strange peolpe to get what you want. 
I wouldn't say that it was not important at all back in the days, the society and the structure of the whole world has changed so much,though, that we really depent on cooperation with people today. Therefore I think its more important today and in the future then it was in the past.
