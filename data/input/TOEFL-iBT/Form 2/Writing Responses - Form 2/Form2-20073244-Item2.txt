	In the past, people could only focus on their individual lives by either harvesting, hunting, or gathering food. However, In the contemporary world, the interaction between human beings have increased, as cooperation between people became a necessity for survival, such as trade between different nations and global business. Thus, I agree with the statement that the ability to cooperate well with others is far more essential in today's world than it was in the past.
	The ability to cooperate well with others is generally necessary in everyday lives. When passing a street, one needs to be cooperative and wait for the traffic light to turn green. Even when listening to a music at one's own house, one needs to be cooperative and respect one's neighbors not to turn up the volume to its highest at night. Likes these, the ability to cooperate well with others is needed everywhere in this society. 
	To be specific, the ability to cooperate well with others is much more important than it was in the past as majority of works demand group works. Unlike in the past when people only had to hunt or harvest for their own food, in today's world, it involves cooperation between people in almost every job. For example, on December 28th, my brother decided to be a math teacher at Washington Junior High. Unlike his anticipation of only teaching students math, cooperation with others was demanded everywhere. Not only did he have to cooperate with students by coming up with a solution when not completing the work, but cooperate with other teachers on planning the intensity of school exam. 
	Furthermore, the ability to cooperate well with others is indispensable in this society, as the world is becoming more globalized. One needs to be cooperative at job, or any other circumstances in everyday lives. Thus, the ability to cooperate well with others is undoubtedly far more important than it was in the past.
