The world we live in now is a very globalized world. You can see it from the business transactions taking place all over the world. It seems that there are no more boundaries between regions, countries, or even continents. Because of that, we more and more need to be able to cooperate well with people all around the world.

The first reason for supporting this idea is the development of internet in the last decade. Since then, we are introduced with a new term, e-commerce. This means that by using internet, we can do business with pretty much everyone in the planet. All the barriers and obstacles that were once impossible to overcome, have now become obsolete.

Second, the trend nowadays is to build a industry specialist company. This means that companies now want to be more focused in doing their business. They will not produce all the raw materials needed for their business. Instead, they will outsource. By outsourcing, it means that they should have and retain good relationship with their suppliers, because if not, it could be the end of their business. 

Finally, as the saying goes "One man can achieve good things, but you need help from others to make it great", business without cooperation would not even be called business at all. Even when you are working, be it with your subordinate, your peer, or even your boss, you know that you need their cooperation in order to do your work properly. Otherwise, you would have to do it all by yourself, which is quite impossible, if I may say so.

All in all, I agree with the statement questioned. Having the ability to cooperate has always been a good skill. But now, it is even more demanded, especially from the business people.
