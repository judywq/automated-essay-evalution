

	The meaning of values and ethics has gone to a distant second place after being substituted by disgusting and horrible thoughts of greed. Why is it so important to cooperate with other people? Why is a lending hand always of great value? 
	We live in a complex and dangerous world. The so called "global village" is in effect. Computers, cellular phones, airplanes, all work together in order to make our world smaller every single day. As thoughts of economic development, educational advantages and sociologial gains come to mind, also the means by which human beings obtain all these becomes very important. In the past, if a person had a small portion of land, he could grow some vegetables and survive engaging in trade with somebody who had cattle. It was not very difficult to succeed if you had the right tools. Nowadays it is different. 
	Children go to school and as soon as they begin kindergarden they are inmersed in an environment made out entirely of competition. Who has more stars? Who is the fastest? Who brushesh his teeth better? These are the things tought to our young ones in order to succeed. However, if somebody gets out of his own way in order to help someone else, to cooperate in reaching a goal he might be called a loser. This is so because the personal goal of this person will not be reached, whereas the goal of the person he helped will be recahed. Personal satisfaction will then be taken into account and society will once again be labeled a loser, instead of a winner because he decided to put his own personal wellbeing aside to help somebody else. 
	Teamwork is important. A common goal in society can help eveybody better their quality of life and forget about our personal issues for a while. In a world like the one we live today it is definetly important to cooperate with others.
