          The lecture is refuting all the things that are said against fish in farming which has become very popular in the society, specially in farms. First of all, fish farming does not jeopardizes the health of wild fish; instead of that, it is jeopardized for commercial wasting which is constantly produced. Secondly, The chemicals used to breed fish in farms have not been proved scientifically to be harmful to humans;on the contrary, scientists have found that fish bred in that way has health benefits to humans. As a matter of fact, fish have less fat and nutritional values compared with other kind of foods which cannot contribute to a heathier society. Finally, the issue regarding the reduction of protein available in the sea due to fish feeding is irrelevant. It is irrelevant because humans do not use that kind of source for living.
