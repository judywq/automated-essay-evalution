    For years, the ability of birds migrating accuratly has puzzled naturalists and it still is. there are several theories for the birds navigational abilities.
   One theory suggests that birds navigate using celestial objects like the Sun or the stars. However, celestial objects are not always visible and can be easily covered by clouds. therefore celestial object cannot be the whole story. 
   Another theory claims that  birds navigate by landmarks like rivers, coastlines, and mountains. However, some scientists placed birds in places very much new for them and they stil had the ability to return.
    A third theory indicates that birds use some type of internal compass that responses to Earth's magnetic field. Still, such a compass could not lead the bird to the exact point of return. Just knowing East from West does not give the accurate point of which the bird is headed. So there must be another ability and form of navigating for birds that would navigate them accuratly .
