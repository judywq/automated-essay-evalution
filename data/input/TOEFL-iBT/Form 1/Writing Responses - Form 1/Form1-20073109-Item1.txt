The author of the reading passage talks about the ability of migrating birds. He explains three theories about it . However, each of the three theories has its limit.
The first theory suggests that birds navigate in reference to celestial objects such as sun or the stars. Some birds may orient their flight with the help of sun's path across the sky. The secend theory  explains that birds navigate by landmarks such as rivers and mountains. What is more, reserchers conclude that migrating birds must use memorization skills. And the third theory claims that birds use compass to help their migration.
The professor of the listening passage thinks that each theory has defects. For the first theory, some birds can also migrate even without sun or star at night, so it can not explain this situation.
For the second theory, some birds can migrate to the place they have never been to, so they must use some other ways except for the landmarks. For the last theory, compass is not enough for some birds' migration.
According to the two passages, the theory of the migration of birds is very complex which is puzzled for many reserchers.
