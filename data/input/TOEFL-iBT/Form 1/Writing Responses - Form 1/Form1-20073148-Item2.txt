        To achieve  in life,one must not just choose a subject of study but choose a subject he or she is interested in.This is a very great factor in determining an achievement in life.If an individual just chooses a subject,the person might end up regretting for the rest of his or her life.
         It is quite alright to choose a subject based on job purpose,but it might be a factor that  will lead to an unsuccesful lifetime.The reason is because when an individual chooses a subject for job or career sake,he or she might not be able to cope with the subject.And later,advised by the school to change course after much years have been wasted.But if the student is wise enough to choose the subject he or she is interested in,the result would be execellent.A good example is Bill Gate a famous computer engineer.Right from his youth age,he loved anything about computer.So he grew up with that interst and studied computer engineering.He performed perfectly due to the love he had for the subject,and that made him one of the richest engineers on earth.
       On the other hand, lets take for example the former Nigerian president General chief Sunny Abacha.He had a potential of becoming a good medical doctor and restore life to the fellow people in his country.But instead,he went to the art department to study law.When he saw the outcome was bad,he joined the military force and later forcefully became the president .He therefore ruled the poeple of his time poorly.Whereas,if had gone for the subject he was interested in,he must have made fame in an unforgetable way.
         So with clear examples,I can confidently say that it is the best any student can imagine to go for the subject he or she is interested in.This indeed would have a profitable end in an historic way.
