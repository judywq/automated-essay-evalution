In the Lecture the limitations of the theories explaining the navigational activities of the the birds are discussed.
As discussed in the passage ,there are three types of theory,and according to the first theory the bird navigates in response to the celestial object.
The second theory states that the birds navigates by landmark.
The third theory states that the birds uses internal compass that response to Earth's magnetic field,and it helps the birds to navigate.
The limitation of these theories as discussed inthe lecture have been discussed below:
The limitation of the first theory is that,it is not necessary that the bird cannot reach its destination if the celestial object like sun or star are not present,because many a times it was seen that whenever the sun or the stars were behind the cloud the bird could reach its destination.
The second theory that the birds navigates by landmarks like river,coastline and mountains are criticised by many researchers, because when they conducted the research by leaving the bird in an unknown place,they observed that the bird could get back to its destination  even without knowing their landmark.
And the third theory that the birds uses a type of internal compass that responds to earth magnetic field,cannot simply account for the birds migration,that is even if the Earth's magnetic field is not sensed by the bird,it still  reaches its destination.
