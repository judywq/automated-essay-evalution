Recently,there is a debate over whether people should choose to study subjects they are interested in or choose subjects to prepare for a job or career.As a matter of fact,the issue of which kind of subjects people should choose is a complex and controversial one.Different people hold different views due to their distinct backgrounds,and whether one has advantages over the other may quite depend on the specific situations.However,there is not a universial answer to this kind of question,so the final judgment should based on a case-by-case analysis.As far as I am concerned,I agree that it is more important to choose to study subjects to prepare for a job or career.
In the first place,we live in a world where knowledge accumulated by multiplying and at the same time becoming more and more specialized.As a normal individual,we do not have the abilities to predict which subject will be the most suitable one for us to live in the world,in which competition is becoming fierce day by day.But the job reflect it to some extent.We can easily draw from the salary to know that the high salary often attached to the job which has a bright furture.
On the other hand,the subjects that we are interested may not reflcet the need in our current lives.Only by persuing the most useful subjects,subjects such as computer,finace,accounting and law,we will have the abilities to overcome different problems we may face everyday.
It may not be wrong to choose to study subjects you are interested in,because you can totally put yourself when studying them and thus get the most happiness.But this alone does not constituted a sufficient support for the issued discussed and therefore are too weak to reach the conclusion.Thus ,we may safely draw the conclusion that choose to study subjects you are interested may not help you to succeed in modern world.
In a word,it is more important to choose subjects to prepare for a job or a career which is most related to your life and therefore is most practical.
