     There are some theories describing navigation for birds's emigration, but the lecture talks about each theory's limitation for birds's migration. 
     Firtst theory suggests that migrating birds use the sun and stars as a map in the day and night times and regurate their location in relation to North Star. However, Those things are not always visible. Therefore, the theory cannot work for whole case of bird's migration.
     In the second theory, it is asserted that birds identify some regional marks such as rivers. In the argue of the lecture, the kind of land mark can not be used in whole situation because migrating birds fly in unknown place, and when the regional place used as a land mark damaged, they cannot find their direction. Therefore, the theory is limmited. 
     Also, the third theory which suggests that migrating birds use internal compass that responds to Earth's magnetic field is limmited one. It is because the compass shows rough direction, and it is not in detail enough to go to a particular place. 
     Since these reasons, gave by the lectre, the three theories suggesting use of stars, the sun, land marks, and compasses are able to state as limmited theories for describing navigation of bird's emigration.
