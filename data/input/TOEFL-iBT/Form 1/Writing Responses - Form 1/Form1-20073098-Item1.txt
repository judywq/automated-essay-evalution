There are three main ideas about bird migration. 
First said that birds find ways by using of some celestrial objects like sun, stars. Some scientists think birds use suns moving way at  day and north stars position at night for consider the way. But this theory has some problems that sun and stars usually shaded by clouds or something. 
Second idea is about birds memories of landmarks like rivers, coastlines, and mountains. But birds find their way well even they passing perfectly new places where they don't remember.
Third thing is about internal compass that respond to Earth's magnetic field. Some scientists guess that crystals or something in bird's body can be used as little compass. But this idea also has weak points that no one can find way by just compass. We should know present location and distance, direction to arrivals to find ways.
As we see, every idea can be a method for finding ways but cannot be whole story. More researchs are needed for final conclusion.
