There is a region on the birds called hippocampal region, and when it gets damaged, the bird cannot use its ability as well, so its memory and ability to navigate gets worse too.
	The third and last theory holds that birds use like an internal compass that responds to Earth's magnetic field. But a bird is not like a human that knows where he is, birds have self-orientation that sometimes fails on them, so that is why they lose track of where they are, soy they get lost and can not use the compass anymore.
