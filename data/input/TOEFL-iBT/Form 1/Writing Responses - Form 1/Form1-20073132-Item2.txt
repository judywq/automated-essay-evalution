     Students have different ways of choosing their subjects. Some would rather take a subject they are interested in, other would rather think of their future and therefore take subjects related to their future career. What is more important?

      Nowadays, students became lazy and only work on the subjects they are interested in. On the one hand, it could be a great idea as they would work more seriously on the subjects that attract their attention. A student that loves what he is doing, spends more time doing his homework and studying than a student that is faced to an uninteresting topic. Picture this, a student that doesn't like the subjects he is studying will find difficulties in doing something he does not like later.

      On the other hand, students today only think of the present moment and are not very concerned in what is waiting for them in the future. Most of children are nowadays interested in things that do not have lots of issues in the future. There are some subjects that are essentials to do a good job in the future. Life has become difficult and students have to learn about all kind of subjects instead of being restricted to a little number of subjects only because they are interested in them.

       To put it into a nutshell, i think students have turned an age where they are enough mature to choose or not what they have to learn. However, they should be advised and told that they must take some subjects that woul help them in the future. To conclude, students should be forced to take some esssential subject but sould also be given the choice to choose other subjects that they are interested in. This accomodation would make them have a balanced life in the future.
