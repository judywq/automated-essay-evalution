      I do agree to the statement that one should always choose to study subjects one is interested in than to choose subjects to prepare for a job or career. Yes, job and career is important in one's life but it is also important for the job to cater to one's priorities and demands. 
       If one chooses to study subjects in which he is genuinely interested, he gets a total hold of that particular area of study, explore new regions and finds new avenues. This automatically will bring him success in the field of study he is involved. On the other hand if one choose to study subjects only to prepare for a job, he may not be successful in that. He will not find any interest in that, hence he will lose willingness to toil and spend time for his studies. This may even hamper his future.
     Furthermore it is important to choose study subjects of your choice because one needs to be spontaneously involved in his studies. One cannot force to study which can be the situation if the subjects are not the one a person prefers. While studying subjects for jobs which are usually very technical compels you to be machinised. They usually don't cater to your brain's needs.
     If a person takes up subjects to study for jobs, he may lose his interest  in his studies since this in not his area of preference. This can really have adverse effects. He may not be able to give his best efforts and best results which could have been in the other case. Rather if one gets trained in the area he is interested in, his profound knowledge and interest will always sustain his willingness to continue to pursue that area. He will never feel bored with his work.
I personally believe that it is very important to be involved in something which is according to one's will. One's heart and brain must synchronize to bring in success and happiness in life.
