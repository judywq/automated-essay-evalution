This is a limitation for their navigational abilities. Fourtunately, they also have ability that allows to memorize landmarks, like rivers, coasrtlines, and mountains. Although, it would not be possilble to navigate,if these regions are damaged, it is one of the important vavigational skills for them. For the last ablility,they use internal compass.
