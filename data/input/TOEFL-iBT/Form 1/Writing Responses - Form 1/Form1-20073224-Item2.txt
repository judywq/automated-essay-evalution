	I think that it is more important to choose study subjects you are interested in rather than to choose subjects that prepare you for a carrer. I think, also, that the subjects you choose guided by what you like are the ones that you are going to use in your carrer because your job will be what you like to do, in most of the cases. 
	For example, if I am able to choose my study subjects, I would choose creative writing. And if I am planning on being a journalist or writer in the future, the subject I chose because I liked and I am interested in will help me prepare for my career as a journalist or a writer.
	However, some people would rather choose study subjects that prepare you for a job because maybe they're not planning on having a job they specifically like. Maybe they are mostly thinking about the money they will be earning. I think this is a mistake because for you to enjoy your life, you should be doing what you like to do in order to wake up happy every morning and eager to get things done. I would not like to imagine the sadness of people that have to wake up every morning to do something you don't enjoy.
	For me, doing what I like is very important. In order for me to be happy, it is essential to be doing something I enjoy and that I know I am going to be confortable doing for the rest of my life. 
	At the end, what decides your success in life will be how much heart you put in your work, based on how much enthusiasm you have to do it, and the feeling that you accomplished something that was succesful. The money you earn will not at all make you happy or buy the feeling of accomplishment you get for doing what you love.
