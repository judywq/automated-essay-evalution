	The first theory is that the birds navigate in reference to celestial objects such as the Sun or the stars. The fact that makes this theory weak is that the Sun and the stars are not always visible because of clouds.
	The second theory is that the birds navigate by landmarks like rivers, coastlines, and mountatins. However, there also is limitation with this theory. When the birds fly to a random place that they have never been accidently, than it is impossible to come back to their home, and it is hard to memorize every landmarks that would be constantly changing.
