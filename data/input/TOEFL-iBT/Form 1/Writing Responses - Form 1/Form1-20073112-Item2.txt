	Some people may have opposite opinion, but I agree with the opinion that choosing to study subjects we are interested in. In my view point, especially undergraduate students are necessary for studing a certain field thet they are interested in, so I consider this problem from its position.  I have following reasons.

	Firstly, it is important for students to do training of logical thinking. I believe that all academics are consists of several logics, and if a student studies a certain academic fields, then he learns not anly the special knowledge but also the logical thinking based on its field. For example, my special field is economics. This field always requires to explain the reason or procedure that imply the conclusion. Thus, by studying economics, I learn the logical thinking. This skill is universally important skill not only in academic field but also real society. In other words, this skill helps us when we face the problem that we do not know how to solve. Therefore, I believe that studying paticular fields is important. 

	Secondly, the preparation for a job or career such as inturnship in the company is also experienced after students enter the company. For instance, a president of Japanese company said that current students tend to be proud of attending many inturnship, but it is not appealing for him.  He considers that such experience in the inturnship is also experienced every people who enter companies, and it is not worth to do during the students. I agree with his comment, and consider that students should do special experience that they can do only in their campus life. I believe that studying a certain field hardly is such an experience.   

	In conclusion, since the aspects of both training and experience, I definitely agree with the opinion that it is more important to choose to study subjects we are interested in than to choose subjects to prepare for a job or career.
