I think if you want to study it is important to study something you are really interested in. 
Of course I can understand that there are people who prefer to study something that will give them the possibility to resiece a well-paid job after their studies. But chances are high that these people later in life will notice that they are not satisfied with the job they have.
The best thing to do, in my eyes, is trying to combine a subject you are interested in with a subject which prepares you for a later career.
I, for example, am a American Studies student. American Studies is my major, and I combine it with Political Science as my minor. I chose American Studies because I am very interested in American literature, culture, history and linguistics.  But I know that it could get difficult for me to find a job in the future. American Studies is nice and a lot of fun but when you try to find a job your boss might ask you what else you have done during your studies because he is probably not too familiar with this subject. That is one reason why I elected Political Science as my minor. The person I will try to convince that no one but me is the right person to do the job in his department can relate much better to a topic like Political Science than to American Studies. And if he or she is able to relate to what I have studied it will be easier to reciece the job. That is at least how it is in Germany. It might be different in the United States but I do not know. 
I also feel better by studing something which will be apreciated in the future. 
Therefore I think of course it it important to study something you will need for your later career. But if you do not like what you are doing it is worthless becaue it is not going to make you happy.
