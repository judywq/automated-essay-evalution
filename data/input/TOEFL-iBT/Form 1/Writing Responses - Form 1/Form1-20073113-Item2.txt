     These days, some young people are so short-sighted that they just want to settle and pick jobs which they could be payed properly even though they do not want those jobs. But in my opinion, it is more crucial to choose to study subjects one is interested in than to choose subjects to prepare for a job or career. 
     One of the reasons I prefer to select the subjects one is interested in is that a person can work more efficiently and passionately. Because it helps one to feel great about what one does and one could put all his passion and energy on this field. And I think that makes people keep going and happy.
     Secondly, there is no serious stress during work time. Stress makes one depressed, tired and sensitive to all kinds of thins surrounding a person. Also, stress get people to wonder if what he is doing is the right thing to do for the rest of his life. If the work is what one had chosen to do and what one really wants, it would not be so stressful. Thus, it is significant to pick the subjects one wants to study.
      Third of all, one could guarantee that he could do it for a long period of time. If one is not sure if he is going to have the job more than just earning money for a short amount of time, one will not be able to settle in one place. Additionally, one will be trapped into the vicious cycle of wondering what one wants. 
     To study and find a job is hard for everyone but those process are necessary for people to grow and to be happy at last. It would be easier to be happy when they can do what they do. Hence, I suggest that people should select to study subjects what they really want to study.
