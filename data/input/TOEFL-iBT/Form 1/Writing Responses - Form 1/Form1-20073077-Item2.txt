	Should I choose the major that i like? or should I choose the major that will prepare me for a good job?
	This question has been asked by many students every year. Whenever they planed to go to a college they will start thinking about this question.
	My opnion about this question is that it depends on the person him self. If the student like a specific subject so much, then he should study it. Because by doing this he will be sure that he will have good grades in that subject, and this will assure him to have a good job since he is the best of his class. Even if he was not sure about the job, at least he did what he felt that he like to do, and it is not neceserly to have a job in company. If he likes what he is studying he can start with a private job by himself and this will be more excited than working in a regular job.
	On the other hand, if the student like a subject but not that much, here I can say that he should choose the other subject that he can find a job after the graduation. Because his feeling might change later, and at that time he will not find a job and he will be in major that he does not like.
	About my self, I like the art field but I choosed to majer in the engineering field, because I'm not sure if I can do well with the art, and I know that it does not have many apportunties to find a job.
	That was my opnion, and I know that every one has another opinions, but at the end it depends on the person him self.
