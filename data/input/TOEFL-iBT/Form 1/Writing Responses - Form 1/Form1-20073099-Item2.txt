    I disagree the statements that it is more important to choose subjects which you are interested in more than to choose subjects related with a job or carrer. I will present several reasons about my disagreements below.
    First when I study a certain subject concerning my future job or career, I could deal with the subject more effectively. I will always think about how my subject will be used for others at the spot where I will work. Usually the job is asscoated with others  For example when I intend to work in the business about computers, I am willing to continue to study on the speed or solidness of the computer for the buyers. In addition to effectiveness, creative thinking happen when I consider the subjects' being used. 
   Second I believe that the knowledge which I currently are acquiring should be revealed to the real activity ulitimately. I remeber that a certain philosopher insists that good knowledge is natually connected with action. The theory is the foundation of American education The job is the place where the abstract knowledge is shown up concretely. 
    Third when someone has stable job or career, he or she could have additional energies to work other things. If someone undergoes hard times because of money, he or she actually  will miss the opportunities to study more subjects. In this sense, the subjects related with the future job could bring the secure ground for people's life in the future.       
    Finally I insists that it is more effective and practical for us to choose subjects to equip for a future job or career. This decision will bring stable position in the future to keep on studying more subjects.
