     The professors is lecturing about some limitations of three theories of bird's possible ways to navigate. 
     We usually cannot rely on the first theory because the Sun and the stars are not always visible. That could make birds impossible to follow the map of the stars and the Sun in the sky. 
     The second theory is also unreliable. Tracing the landmarks like rivers, coastlines and mountains is sometimes hard for the birds to find the way back home. And the locations of landmarks could be changed after the birds leave certain area. When they are try to come back home, they are going to have difficulty in searching for certain places. So they will be confused between their memories and the present situation.
     The limitation of the third theory is that the internal compass of the birds is not enough to find their exact homes. It means that sensing the directions is not achievable enough for the birds to navigate particular places.
