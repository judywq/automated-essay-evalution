I think choosing subjects what I'm interested is more important. Of course some one said that considering job first and then taking subjects fit in the job is more good for someone's future. But I think this idea is not good because when we choose career, we usually tend to prefer socially high-level jobs, or careers such as doctors, lawyers, or entertainers that can earn much wealth and honor. There are no interests, no dreams. And in this case, many people soon loose interest about their job and cannot get good scores for working. However, when we take our more interested subject and study, we can find courage, joyness and future vision to go on while taking the subject. We can choose, made our future by our own hand. Because we have interest, we can have happy working mind, and this made us to do our job more effectively. In my opinion, choosing job first and fit ourselves to it is just like walking down through the choosen way by other peoples will, not ours.Choosing career should be done after finding our interest and will for future way. With no interest and vision of future, there can be no happy, and wealthy life physically, and also mentally.
