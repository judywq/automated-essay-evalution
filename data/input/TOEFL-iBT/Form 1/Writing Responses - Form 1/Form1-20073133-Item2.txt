   In my opinion it is very important to choose a subject of your interest to study rather than to choose subject that prepares you for job or career.
   Everyone has a childhood dream to become someone like  to become a doctor or pilot, which develops into a person's interest as he grows. If one choses to opt for the same field of study as his  interest, he  will think that he is heading towards his dream becoming true and will put in all efforts to achieve this goal, if not it may end up in a frustrated life.
   On the other hand if one choses to take up subjects for career sake without having any interest in that subject he may not have any contentment even though he may get a job. He will always have the regret of not taking a subject of his interest.
   I can quote an example from my own experience, my brother always wanted to be a doctor, but he happened to take up maths and got   into engineering, because my dad believed that Engineers have a better chance to get a job than doctors. Finally my brother became an Engineer, but till now whenver he comes across a doctor or visits hospital, he says -I wish I was a doctor, I could also have served people. Now he has plans to take up Medicine somehow and change his career which may or may not be possible. 
  I believe one should always do what interests him, and when you do things of your interest it is shown in the quality of your work and gives you appreciation and fame, resulting in more hard work and better work everytime. This becomes a cycle. One should be focussed in life to determine what his interests are and head towards achieving that goal and persue a career in that field.Success comes to those who work hard,  and not merely by taking up a subject which will fetch you a job.
