I agree with the statement that,"It is more important to choose to study subjects you are interested in than to choose subjects to prepare for a job or career".
I strongly support the above statement, as it is always said,that you can do the best in the subject you are interested in,a very good example for this is a situation of the teenager while choosing his/her major,for continuing with the further education.
In many cases we see, that the child is often interested in some subject,but the parents and gaurdians feels that if he/she takes up a particullar subject as major,then it would be better for his/her future,career wise.
And it is in such situation that the child is forced to take up the subject ,he/she is not interested in and due to which the person cannot do well in the study, and as a result they might drop off and land nowhere,whereas if the child was allowed to take the subject of his or her choice then,he/she would have studied the subject with interest and could land up in the very good job in the field of his or her study and interest.
I personally had the same type of experience when i finished my high school with the very good percentage of marks.My main interest was on humanities,but my parents and gaurdians,asked me to take up science as my major subject,and as I was really not interested in it,so i couldn't put in my hundred percent effort,and as a result of which grade point came down drastically, then i meet with a student counsellor of my school ,and she suggested me to talk with my parents frankly about the matter.
I discussed the matter seriously with my parents, and lucklily,they understood my diificulties, and  allowed me to change my major.
This was the biggest achievment for me, and as i had chose the subject of my interest, so I could put in my hundred percent,and was able to excell in my subject, and am now sure that I have a flourishing career waiting for me after my studies.And the very year, I changed my major I topped my class,so  this illustrates the point that it is more important  to choose to study the subject you are interested in then to choose subject to prepare for a job or career.
 Thus I would like to conclude by saying that,"if one is an expert in one's own field then it won't be difficult for a person to get into the good jobs or career,in field of one's own interest."and it is always good to study the subject you are interested in.
