I believe it is very crucial to study a subject which I am interested in because part of being successful is to try to find ways that can enhance my ability to receive information and analyse it, to enjoy the process of going through that. 

This issue is most reflected in the period after finishing school, when the student is heading to college and thinking of the best and most convenient path to study. The amount of subjects and options to choose from can be very confusing, so the best way is to choose a path or a subject that you know you will enjoy studying. 

From my experience, I have applied this method and I chose to study Business Administration, a field which I have always wanted to research about, so one day I can become a successful businessman, and it worked. During classes I was focused and I have always interacted positively with my proferssors. I used to enjoy those field trips to diffirent companies and factories, where we accumulated a long list of inquiries and had them delivered in class for dicussion and brainstorming.

Some students have their study path chosen for them, either by one of their parents. This is very unhealthy and can cause really damaging results. The student will be distracted during classes, will not focus his attention, and in the end he will have mininum exposure to the chosen field resulting in wasting college years.

Every small aspect should be taken into consideration while studying and the interest aspect is the most imperative as I have explained above. So every person should indulge himself in this process and try his best to utilize his time to the best of his interests.
