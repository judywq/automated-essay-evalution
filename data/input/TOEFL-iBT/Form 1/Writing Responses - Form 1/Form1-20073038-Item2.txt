Base on my personal opinion i prefer to study subjects i am interested about rather than to choose subjects that i don't really like. I prefer to study subjects that i am interested in for two main reasons.

First of all, when you choose to study subjects that you really like it you are more enthusiastic about it. You retain knowledge easyly than studying subjects that you dislike. For example, once i choosed to study music, because is something that i really enjoy i learned to play the guitar faster than other students that were in the same class than me because their parents considered that study music will help them, but not really because they wanted to do it.

Secondly , when you study subjects interesting to you. You would be able to practice it or later on teach about the subject that you studied. You would do your activities with love and not  just for simple compromise.

In our days, society push you to study something because it will be good for your  future. How many times i have seen people that work or choose a career only because they can earn a lot of money or it is the career that somebody else wants, such as family or friends but not them who should be the only ones to care about it. These kind of people will be suffering the rest of their lives, they are not really happy, they don't enjoy their jobs or their careers, they can also fall in depression or they will try to find ways to do what they  wanted at the beginning.

All in all, studying subjects of your own interest will give you more quality of life, like enjoy what you really do and to maintain your hapinness .
