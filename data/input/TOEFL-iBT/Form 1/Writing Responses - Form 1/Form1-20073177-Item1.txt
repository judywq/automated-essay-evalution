   There are three theories proposed to account for the bird's navigatinal abilities.But,unfortunatley none of them explains all the situtations of bird's navigating to different places.
       For example the first theory says that birds navigate in reference to celestial objects like Sun or the stars,giving some evidences saying that some of the birds migrate by day stay going along the Sun's path across the sky.This can be ruled out by a simple case where sky is full of clouds,in such case birds can't find any celestial objects to follow and migrate accordingly.
       Where as the second theory which says that birds navigate by having some landmarks like rivers,coastlines, and mountains.But if this were correct birds cannot migrate when they are brought to a new location,which is not true in reality.
       Coming to third theory it says that bird's have Magnetite in their beaks, which has magnetic properties.So this serves as a compass when alligned with earth's magnetic field.But this theory does not account properly because compass can only show direction but not the exact place where birds actually wants to migrate.
