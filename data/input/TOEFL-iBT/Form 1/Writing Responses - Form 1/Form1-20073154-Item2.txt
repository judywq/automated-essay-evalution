The issue at hand is; Is it more important to choose subjects of our interest or subjects related to our career? This is a very important issue because our future depends on the foundation of the initial education. I prefer choosing the subjects, which will aid my career in the future.

The courses we choose directly effect kind of knowledge a person comprehends. Moreover, this knowledge background is reused and implemented during the construction of our career. Hence, choosing career related courses is always advantageous because they have a direct impact on our future understanding and efficiency in the career.

Another reason as to why i prefer choosing career related courses is; when we apply for under graduate education, these courses become the basic reason for the acceptance of our application. So, indirectly career related courses work as the ticket or a validity pass for our first step in the career.

Furthermore, having a strong background information about the concerned topic builds up a strong backbone for the upcoming education at undergraduate or graduate schools. We feel ahead of the other students and can even have a better understanding for the topic. For exmaple--in a biology lecture at university, a student who has taken science courses during highschool will feel superior and will be more efficient than a student having a sociological background. 

In colcusion, as shown in this essay. Selection of career related or career recomended courses boosts up our confidence and allows us to aspire towards a succesful and a bright future.
