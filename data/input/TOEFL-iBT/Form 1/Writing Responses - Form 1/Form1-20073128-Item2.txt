 Everyone hopes to make a decision that will benefit himself or herself the most. However,  it is hard to do so sometimes. You may face to choose between a way that will better your social life or a way that you prefer to. At this time, I believe it is more important to choose to study subjects you are interested in than to choose subjects to prepare for a job or career. In my opinion, I think people were born to enjoy their lives, and choosing subjects that you are interested in studying is a way to enjoy you life. Although we have to think about the "real" problems in our life, it is not a good reason for making us to give up our "natural right." If you study hard on the subjects you like, you will not only enjoy them but also have chance to expert on them. When you expert on something, you don't really to worried about how to keep your life going because chances are everywhere. There is no such subject that can gurantee you to have a good job. Also, there is no such thing called "The best job." "The best job" is only in your own heart. If you think that it is what you like or  what you want, you should stick to it. You can still have a nice life  by choosing subjects you like. Buttom line, a good life is only for the people who know what they want and try to work hard.
