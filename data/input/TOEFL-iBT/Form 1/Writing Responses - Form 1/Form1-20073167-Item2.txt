              Choose the Subject that You are Really Interested in

"Every subject  creates  suceessful person."That is an acient Chinese saying,which means the subject you choose for own interests,and take it seriously,you can get success someday.According to my view,the interests are more important than the job preparation when you choosing subjects.

The first reason for me to prefer the interests is that interests can stimulate one to study well.When I was in  school,math is my headache,and I have to put much more time to review it than other subjects and the result was I still got a low score in the finals.On the contrary,history is my favorite ,so it's just a piece of cake to study and prepare for the exams,and I always got the highest score in class.This illustrates that the interests is a better dynamic power to let one do things well.

The next point is that if one likes study something ,he or she may find more information about it and to broaden his view of the subject.One of my friend likes painting every much,and then she gave up the chance to go to the best university in my country but went to pursue her dream in Paris.Now she becomes a professional dress designer.She realized that beautiful dresses can make her painting dream come true.Not only the painting skills ,but also she studies physics and maths,even movies by herself,who can say she is not successful in career?

The definition of job and career subject maybe is limited nowadays.MBA has hot subjects which lead many people to study in order to earn more money in future,but who can predict it is really a gold one forever for hunting a job?Remember the Information Technology was booming several years ago,but then,it faded and replaced by others.That's why we can not just study subject just for a job or career for now ,we can't garantee the job after studying the subject.The job should be useful and fit for the whole society.

Therefore,the reasons that I listed above supply my evidence to support that it is more important to chose to study subjects you are intersted in than to choose subjects to prepare for a job or career.
