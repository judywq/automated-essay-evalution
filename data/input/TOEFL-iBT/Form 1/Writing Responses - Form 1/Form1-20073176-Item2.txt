       There is a controversial topic about choosing to study subjects we are interested or choosing subjects to prepare for a job or career. Some people will choose to study what they are interested while other people will choose to study for the future jobs. However, in my opinior, I prefer to study for my own interests. My arguments are listed as follows.
        
In the first place, students who study for interests  can do well in what they learns. For example, when I was young I was interested in drawing.  So I worked hard on it and after a period, many people found that my drawing was excellent. As far as I am concerned, it is wise to study for interests.
      
In the second place, studing for interests can inspire our creactivity. Sometimes, such creaactivity will help to their jobs. For instance, one of my friend likes to sing. Now he is a leader in a company. when he gives out his appointment to his employees, he sings about his requirements. And his employees like this method and think he is a very humorous person. Finally, my friend lead the company very well. So studying for interests sometimes will bring you a big surprise and will give you a bright future.
    
  Adimittedly, there are many advantages to choose subjects to prepare for a job. However, there are many disadvantages,too. For example, people who studying just for career will lose their interest for life  and they will feel that life is grey. In addition, some people will also lose confidence if they work hard on these subjects but finally they do not have a good job.
    
  In conclusion, It is sagacious to study subjects we are interested in. than to choose subjects to prepare for a job.
