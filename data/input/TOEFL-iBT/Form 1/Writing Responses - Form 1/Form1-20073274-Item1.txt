The lecture stressed the fact that none of the three theories could explain the birds ability exclusivly. The first one that mentioned the sun or the north star as references for the birds orientation system is limited by the fact that neither the sun nor the stars are visible when clouds or dust are hiding them. The second theorie referring to the memorization skills of birds is not reliable alone because there had been various examples of the returning of birds to their home even if they were brought to a place they have never been before. After all even the last one that mantains the existance of a magnatic mineral called "Magnetite" in the birds beaks that interacts with the magnetic fields of the earth to secure orientation is weak. Because, so the argument of the lecturer, at first the birds would need to know their exact position from which they can "calculate" their further migrating route.
