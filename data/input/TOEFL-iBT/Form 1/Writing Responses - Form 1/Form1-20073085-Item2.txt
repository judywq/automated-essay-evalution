	It is a common belief that demands and expectations in nowadays life have increased a lot. In daily life we have to deal with different subjects and difficulties and we have to be prepared to face them. In order to achieve something like this we have to study a lot. To my mind it is better to choose subjects to prepare for a job or a career than to study subjects we are interested in. 
	Let's imagine that somebody wants to work in an organization as a Manager Director or as an Account Manager. In both situations he has to be able to face a numerous of problems that may appear during his working. By studying subjects that are similar to these kind of jobs, he will be definitely reinforce his capability to find the most appropriate solutions to the upbreaking problems. 
	In addition to the above statement, while somebody studies subjects to prepare for a job, makes him an expert in this particular job's field. He will be able to learn things that under different circumstances he wouldn't have been able to. When choosing to study subjects relatively to the job you want to do, you give a chance to yourself to become better and better on this particular task. 
	Furthermore, we have to keep in mind that our future is based on our job. How many times haven't you admitted that you want to choose a particular job not only because you like it, but also because it gives you the opportunity to live under better conditions? In order somebody to achieve this targit he needs to get better and better in his job's fields and requirements. Choosing subjects that have to do with his preparation for a job or a career will have a good effect on him and his future.
	All in all, choosing subjects to prepare for a job or a career will lead somebody to become an expert on the job/career he wants to follow. Being the best one, out of many, helps somebody to achieve a more comfortable and nicer life with no worries.
