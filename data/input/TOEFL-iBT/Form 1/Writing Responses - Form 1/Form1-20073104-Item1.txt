The second theory explains that birds use memorization skills, such as remenbering landmarks, to navigate; but that theory does not explains what happen when birds are taken to other places with different landmarks and still, they kwon where to go by the time of migration.
The third and last theory claims that birds use a type of internal compass that responds to Earth's magnetic field, but the question is how  that internal compass tell them where they are, where they want to go and how far is it.
Basically none of those theories are able to explain what mecanism the birds uses to migrate; maybe not just one but all together explains the ability of migrating birds.
