To lead the market or to follow the market is the big question in student's mind before choosing what to study. 

Many students lost the way during the process to find what they like or what they are interested in. At present, the consumerism and market oriented development implicitly control the directions of labor markets. 

It is interesting to justify whose preparation, the market's preparation or student's preparation in choosing the subjects to study.

Accordingly, the academic institution has designed and outlined the study course to serve for the needs of the market. As a result, the academic will produce a huge quantities of students rather than high qualified students. 

At present, my home country has a lot of students who graduated in the field of marketing, business administration and mangement which getting higher along with the rates of unemployment. In opposite way, the numbers of qualified academic institutions in marketing, business administration and mangement are decreasing. Moreover, my country's economic development still at the level of 'techonlogyless" which has only high growth level but development is unevem

To me, choosing subjects according to what I am interested in would lead me to the right direction since what I do will respond accordingly and effectively to what I am interested in. I believe that the driving forces which come from the core of my expection will be more powerful than the driving forces which driven from the need of market. As a result, the chosen subjects accroding to my interests will accumulate more contents and knowledge and finally, I will have choices in what to do and choose the career to do. Explaning in marketing's point of view, we will see that having more contents will increase the diversity of knowledge which positively encourage and contribute to the market's prosperity.

Therefore, to choose the subjects accordingly your interests will give greater accumulation for your knowledge and public.
