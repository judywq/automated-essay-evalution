In Japan, where I live, some people think that it is more important to choose subjects to prepare for a job or career. However I do not think so. I agree with the opinion that it is important to choose to study subjects I am intereted in for the following reasons.

First, the more interested in that sublject I am, the better outputI can make. For example, when I was in a Keio university, private university in Tokyo, Japan, I studied art history well and I can get the best grade. The reason why I could study so hard is that I liked to study art history. So I read many books and went to a lot of museums. It takes long time to study art. It is possible for me to make effort a lot because I was interested in art history. On the other hand, I hated math, so I did not study so well. Of course, I could not get good scores. Thus, it is important to get good score that I am interested in the subjects. 

Second, it is rare chance to be able to study interesting subjects in our life. After school days, I have to study about the subject even if I am not interested in it  for the job and career. There are not such an  opportunity to to be able to study interesting subjects.


Therefore, I strongly think it is important to choose to study subjects I am intereted in than to choose the subjects for the job and career.
